Our Mission

Empowering Professionals
for a Sustainable Future

Founded in 2015 and headquartered at 207 Regent Street, London, London Petro Academy was built on a single conviction: that the energy industry's most complex challenges are best met by professionals who combine deep technical knowledge with commercial and legal acumen.

We design and deliver intensive, practice-focused training programmes that equip delegates from NOCs, IOCs, energy ministries and financial institutions with the skills to negotiate better contracts, structure stronger joint ventures, manage fiscal risk, and navigate the energy transition.

"We do not teach theory. We equip professionals with the tools, frameworks and confidence to operate at the highest level in global energy markets."

Every programme is led by practitioners with decades of front-line industry experience — lawyers, economists, commercial negotiators and technical experts who have worked on the transactions and policies that define the modern energy landscape.

Our Story

A Decade of Excellence

2015
Founded in London
London Petro Academy Limited incorporated and headquartered at 207 Regent Street, London. First programmes launched in upstream petroleum law and fiscal regimes, serving clients across the UK, Middle East and West Africa.
2017
International Expansion — Middle East & Asia
Established first delivery partnerships in Dubai and Singapore, extending our reach into the Gulf Cooperation Council markets and the Asian LNG trading hubs. Launched the Gas & LNG Contract Negotiations masterclass.
2019
Brazil Market Entry
Launched a dedicated Brazil programme portfolio, covering Production Sharing Contracts, Joint Ventures and fiscal aspects of Brazilian petroleum operations. Rio de Janeiro became a flagship delivery location serving Petrobras and the broader pre-salt supply chain.
2022
Downstream & Renewables Portfolio
Launched our downstream and renewables training catalogue — including commercial refining economics, crude oil markets, renewable energy finance and corporate PPAs — reflecting the industry's strategic shift toward the energy transition.
2026
40+ Programmes Across 5 Continents
LPA now delivers more than 40 specialist programmes annually across London, Rio de Janeiro, Dubai, Singapore, Bali, Bangkok, Doha and virtual platforms — with a faculty of world-class practitioners and a client base spanning over 30 countries.
