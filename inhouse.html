Why In-House

Training that fits your organisation,
not the other way around

Open-enrolment courses are built for the average delegate. In-house programmes are built for your team — your assets, your challenges, your strategic priorities.

Benefit 01

Fully Tailored Content

Every module, case study and exercise is adapted to your sector, assets and business context. We work from your real-world scenarios — not generic examples.

Benefit 02

Cost-Effective at Scale

Training 10 or more colleagues? In-house delivery dramatically reduces the per-delegate cost compared to sending individuals to open programmes.

Benefit 03

Your Location, Your Schedule

We come to you — whether that's your Houston office, a training centre in Lagos, or a five-star hotel in Singapore. Any date, any city, any format.

Benefit 04

Flexible Format & Pace

Classroom, virtual, or blended. Intensive 2-day sprint or a week-long immersive. Morning sessions only? We adapt to your team's availability.

Benefit 05

Combine Multiple Courses

Build a bespoke multi-module curriculum from our portfolio of 38+ courses. Mix upstream, downstream and renewables content into a single integrated programme.

Benefit 06

Certificate of Completion

Every delegate receives a London Petro Academy Certificate of Completion. Available co-branded with your organisation's logo on request.
