About This Course

Course Overview

Brazil presents one of the most complex and opportunity-rich petroleum regimes in the world. For professionals involved in upstream oil and gas projects, understanding how petroleum agreements and joint ventures operate within Brazil's legal framework is critical to success.

This intensive four-day course provides a structured, practical understanding of how Brazil's petroleum regime shapes contracts, investment decisions, and joint venture structures. Participants will explore Brazil's constitutional foundations and institutional structure — in particular, the key roles of the ANP, Petrobras, and Pré-Sal Petróleo S.A. (PPSA) in managing production sharing arrangements.

The training explains how government take mechanisms operate within Brazilian PSCs — covering cost recovery, profit-oil sharing, royalty regimes, and the taxation framework applied to upstream projects. Participants will learn how to balance legal compliance with financial performance across all PSC disciplines.
